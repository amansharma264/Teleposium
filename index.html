About The Event

Teleposium is the flagship event organized by the Department of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering (ETE), bringing together students, professionals, and innovators from various fields.

This mega event features a variety of exciting competitions, open to participants from all colleges. It's a platform to showcase talent, foster creativity, and connect with like-minded individuals and forward-thinking minds.

With a range of attractive prizes and opportunities for networking, Teleposium offers an experience that blends competition, collaboration, and innovation.
